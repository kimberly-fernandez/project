Skillet Chicken Piccata

Piccata describes meat, usually veal or chicken, that is sliced, dredged in flour, then sautéed in a sauce containing lemon juice, butter, and capers. Piccata is an Italian word, the feminine form of the word piccato, meaning “larded”. It is a translation of the French piqué (sharp, as in "piquant"), participle of piquer.
